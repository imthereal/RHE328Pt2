What are succulents?

Succulents are a type of plant that stores water in their leaves. They do well in dry, warm weather. They also require little watering. These properties make succulents very drought-friendly and very easy to care for!

What is SimplySucc?

SimplySucc was founded in 2018 for green-thumbs with busy lifestyles. We strive to have the simplest resources for succulent care. All of our resources are quick, easy, and fun to read.

All our information is curated by the SimplySucc team from years of experience and extensive research. To learn more about our team, look below!
